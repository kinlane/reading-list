Anti-plagiarism software company iParadigms LLC confirmed Monday that it has reached a deal to sell itself to private-equity firm Insight Venture Partners for $752 million, including debt.The Wall Street Journal reported earlier Monday that Insight was nearing a deal to acquire iParadigms from private-equity firm Warburg Pincus.GIC, Singapores sovereign-wealth fund, is joining Insight Venture Partners in the deal.
