The scheme provides monetary incentives for councils to publish data on specific themes in standard tabular formats.The incentive scheme pages document schemas for the tabular data formats and describe the scheme in more detail.The submissions page shows submissions under the scheme and lets you make one for your council.Schemas define the required tabular data format for each of these themes For further information, use our help pages link to or the forum in the Knowledge Hub Local Open Data Community.
