Welcome to the second installment of our government transparency report, where we explain in (hopefully not boring) detail the government requests we receive for account information, and how we respond to those requests.How many requests?From January to June 2014, we provided information—either user data or blog content—for 84% of the requests made, covering 199 blog URLs.This represents approximately 0.00010% of all blogs on Tumblr.
