Tags Drones, Law, Planes, Police, Security, USA The Los Angeles Police Department is waiting for approval before it begins using a pair of recently acquired unmanned aerial vehicles to monitor events in Southern California, but a local man has already beaten them to the punch.Forty-two-year-old Daniel Saulmon of Torrance, CA has been using a camera-equipped drone of his own during the last month or so to get a bird’s-eye-view of area happenings from high above the ground.Coupled with a passion for photographing police activity, Saulmon’s unusual hobby has made him well known among local law enforcement officials.“If there’s police activity in my area that’s close by, I generally will go and try to record it and document what I see,” Saulmon recently told local news network KTLA.“I don’t want to say the police don’t supervise themselves, but in a way there might be a little bit of truth to that,” he added.
