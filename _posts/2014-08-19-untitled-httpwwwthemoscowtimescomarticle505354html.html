Russia is demanding that Bulgaria try harder to prevent vandalism of Soviet monuments, after yet another monument to Soviet troops in Sofia was spray-painted, ITAR-Tass reported.The Russian Embassy in Bulgaria has issued a note demanding that its former Soviet-era ally clean up the monument in Sofias Lozenets district, identify and punish those responsible, and take exhaustive measures to prevent similar attacks in the future, the news agency reported Monday.The monument was spray-painted on the eve of the Bulgarian Socialist Partys celebration of its 123rd anniversary, the Sofia-based Novinite news agency reported.The vandalism was the latest in a series of similar recent incidents in Bulgaria — each drawing angry criticism from Moscow.Early this year, unknown artists painted another monument to Soviet troops in Sofia in the colors of the Ukrainian flag.
