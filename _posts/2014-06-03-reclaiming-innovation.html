If any concept should be seen as an uncomplicated good thing in higher education, its innovation.Defined by a common-sense notion of doing things better and burnished by the sheen of dazzling technological advances, whats not to like about innovation?Yet as 2014 churns on, the glow is wearing off.Today, innovation is increasingly conflated with hype, disruption for disruptions sake, and outsourcing laced with a dose of austerity-driven downsizing.Call it innovation fatigue.
