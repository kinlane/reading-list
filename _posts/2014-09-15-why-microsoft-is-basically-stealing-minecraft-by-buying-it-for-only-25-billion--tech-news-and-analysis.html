So the much-rumored acquisition of Minecraft has finally come to pass, with Microsoft buying the game’s parent company Mojang for $2.5 billion, and no doubt many people unfamiliar with the game are wondering whether the software giant has lost its collective mind.How could a single game with pixelated, low-res graphics be worth that much?It doesn’t even have spaceships!And yet, those who have seen the effect the game has on kids from nine to nineteen figure that Microsoft has effectively stolen Minecraft by paying only $2.
