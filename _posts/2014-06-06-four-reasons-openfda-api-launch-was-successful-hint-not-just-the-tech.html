My friends over at Social Health Insights, wrote about their experience being one of the beta users for the new OpenFDA API, and what they thought contributed to the success of the API launch from the Food and Drug Administration.Getting Feedback Early - The openFDA team solicited feedback early on in the API development process from end users and consumers of the API.This feedback was listened to and ultimately helped shape a very nice API at launch.Being Collaborative - If you are going to do #1 then you must be willing to be collaborative.From the get-go, the openFDA team collaborated with a number of stakeholders and was open all the feedback that streamed in.
