Women are underrepresented in Congress in more ways than one, a new analysis by the Sunlight Foundation finds: Of the more than 5,000 witnesses called to testify before committees of the U.S.House of Representatives during the current congressional session, fewer than one quarter have been female.The findings come courtesy of data recently made available by the Clerk of the House, which Sunlight is providing in developer-friendly format via a newly expanded Congress API.As of Sept.
