Docker is a copybook case study of an open source project done right.The initiative took an existing technology and approach (Linux containers), modernized it for today’s applications and put it out into the community.And my how the community responded, support for Docker is incredible – large vendors and small startups are embracing the project as potentially the biggest thing in infrastructure since virtualization.But amidst this flurry of attention there were questions around how Docker the business (confusingly Docker the project is driven by Docker the company) was going to create a sustainable business out of the project.The company had announced some support solutions but people were looking for more.
