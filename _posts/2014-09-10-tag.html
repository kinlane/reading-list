This year at the API Strategy & Practice Conference (APIStrat) in Chicago (Sept.24–26) we are trying something completely new - the API Speed Hack.Think of the Speed Hack as a “performance enhanced” hackathon.We think it’s a very effective, practical and fun way to promote APIs to developers.There will be eight API providers you might recognize — Twilio, Twitter, Context.
