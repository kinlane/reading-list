Twitter has emerged as one of the most popular social networks in the world.Billions of tweets present an unfiltered view of public opinions and experiences.Researchers can deduce a lot from those 140 characters -- from tracking disease outbreaks to predicting social unrest.Unlike other social networking sites, Twitter provides multiple application programming interfaces that allow for real-time data streaming.Metadata from the stream can even include geolocations that pinpoint the exact geographic coordinates of a user.
