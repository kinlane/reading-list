NYC Taxis: A Day in the Life This visualization displays the data for one random NYC yellow taxi on a single day in 2013.See where it operated, how much money it made, and how busy it was over 24 hours.A Special Thanks goes out to Mapbox and Heroku for generously sponsoring this project to cover the recent surge of activity.This civic technology project visualizes taxi trip data from 2013, showing the activities of a single taxi on a single day.The original data include ~170 Million trips.
