IndieAuth is a way to use your own domain name to sign in to websites.It works by linking your website to one or more authentication providers such as Twitter or Google, then entering your domain name in the login form on websites that support IndieAuth.Why IndieAuth?IndieAuth is part of the IndieWeb movement to take back control of your online identity.Instead of logging in to websites as you on Twitter or you on Facebook, you should be able to log in as just you.
