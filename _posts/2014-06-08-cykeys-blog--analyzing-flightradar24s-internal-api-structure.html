Flightradar24 is a flight tracking service that provides someone with real-time data about thousands of aircraft around the world.It is based on a technology called ADS-B.If an aircraft has an onboard ADS-B transponder, it will broadcast its location (among other things) on the 1090MHz frequency.Recently, many people have been using an rtl-sdr to listen for ADS-B data.Flightradar24 has an app for iOS, Android, Windows Phone, etc.
