While there are many things I don’t agree with Google about, but they are pioneers on the Internet, and in some cases have the experience to lead in some very important ways.In this scenario I’m thinking about Google Account management, and how it can be used as blueprint for all other Software as a Service (SaaS) applications.During a recent visit to my Google account manager, I was struck by the importance of all the tools that were made available to me.Account Settings Google gives you the basic level control to edit your profile, adding, updating the information you feel is relevant.Security Google gives you the password level control, but then steps up with security with 2-step verification, and application specific passwords.
