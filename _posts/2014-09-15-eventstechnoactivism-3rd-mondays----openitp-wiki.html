Techno-Activism Third Mondays (TA3M) is an informal meetup designed to connect software creators and activists who are interested in censorship, surveillance, and open technology.Currently, TA3M are held in various cities throughout the world, with many more launching in the near future.*If you are interested in hosting your own 3rd Mondays, contact SandraOrdonez [at] openitp [dot] org.Please visit each city page for details about location and time.Please be patient since some times the city pages are not updated simultaneously.
