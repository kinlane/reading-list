Discovering private APIs with Charles.app posted 12 July 2014 Nowadays, so many companies have apps, and they have to get their data from somewhere.This has opened a whole new world of (quasi-)open data.By using a proxy to intercept the request made by these mobile apps, you get access to often incredible private APIs.Ive built some great stuff this way, from Pug-o-matic to my Rap Genius ruby gem to my latest project, an availability finder for British Airways air mile redemptions.
