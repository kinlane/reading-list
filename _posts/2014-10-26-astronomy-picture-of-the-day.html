Discover the cosmos!Each day a different image or photograph of our fascinating universe is featured, along with a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer.2014 October 30 Explanation: Frightening forms and scary faces are a mark of the Halloween season.They also haunt this cosmic close-up of the eastern Veil Nebula.The Veil Nebula itself is a large supernova remnant, the expanding debris cloud from the death explosion of a massive star.
