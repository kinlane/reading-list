Docker announced today that it has acquired Orchard Laboratories, the company behind the Fig.The acquisition will bring Orchard’s CEO Ben Firshman and cofounder Aanand Prasad in-house to work on other projects.Fig will be brought into Docker as well, where it will now grow and evolve as a Docker project.The Orchard hosting service offered by Orchard Laboratories, however, will be discontinued.Fig is an open-source project for building and running isolated development environments with Docker.
