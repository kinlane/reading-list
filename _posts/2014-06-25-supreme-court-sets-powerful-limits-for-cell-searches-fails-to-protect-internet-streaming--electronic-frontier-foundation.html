San Francisco - The U.S.Supreme Court issued two big rulings in important technology cases today.In a groundbreaking decision on cell phone privacy, the court set powerful limits for police searches of cell phones, ruling in two consolidated cases that law enforcement must get a warrant before accessing the data on an arrested persons cell phone.The Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF) filed amicus briefs in both of the cell phone search cases that were at issue in todays decision.
