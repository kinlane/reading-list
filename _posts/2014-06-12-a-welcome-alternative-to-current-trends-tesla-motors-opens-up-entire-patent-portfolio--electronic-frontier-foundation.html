The last few years have seen many examples of patents hindering, rather than helping innovation.Patent trolls run rampage while some big companies spend more money on patent wars than research and development.This is why it is so encouraging when companies commit to openness, ensuring their patents do not obstruct future innovation.Today Tesla Motors announced it is opening up its patent portfolio.Founder Elon Musk wrote that the company will not initiate patent lawsuits against anyone who, in good faith, wants to use [Teslas] technology.
