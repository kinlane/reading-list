Last week I noted the push to get Zephyr Teachout to run for NY Governor against the (disappointing to reformers at least) incumbent, Andrew Cuomo (D).Yesterday, as Buzzfeed reports, Zephyr and her running mate, Tim Wu, have announced that the push has worked.Teachout and Wu will challenge the Governor in the Democratic Primary this September.Adding Wu to the law professor ticket will strike pols as odd — not exactly the definition of balance.But the move adds cred to this Teddy Roosevelt revival: Tim is a trust-buster, one of his generations brightest technology scholars, and fearless in his attacks on the monopoly powers that have emerged on the Net.
